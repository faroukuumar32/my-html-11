Egg roll

Ingredients Quantity
Baking powder 1½ tbsp
Margarine 2 ½ tbsp
Water 8-9 tbsp
Eggs (boiled) 7
small eggs 2
Sugar 1½ tbsp
Salt 1 tbsp
Nutmeg 1½ tbsp

Step 1: In a large bowl, mix in flour, baking powder, salt, sugar and nutmeg.

Step 2: Use your hands (clean fingers only please) to blend Margarine into the flour until it’s very crumbly.

Step 3: Crack the eggs and beat them, then mix. The dough should look dry at some point.

Step 4:Start introducing water little by little and work the dough till it is soft and sticky but still quite workable.

Step 5: Work the dough on a clean surface sprinkled with flour for about a minute, put it in a bowl then cover it and let it rest for about 20 minutes.

Step 6: Work the dough again for like a minute then divide it into 7.

Step 7: Tightly wrap each dough around each egg.

Step 8:Fry on medium heat, turn it over regularly until it is golden brown.

Step 9: Serve and enjoy your egg roll.

Nigerian Buns

Ingredients
All-purpose Flour
Sugar
Baking powder
Salt
Eggs
Butter
Milk
Vegetable oil

Step 1: First, gather your ingredients.

Step 2: Next, combine the dry ingredients: flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl and mix well.

Step 3: Add the wet ingredients: milk, eggs, and melted butter, and stir until combined.

Step 4: Heat enough oil in a large but fairly deep skillet over medium heat until the oil is about 350 degrees Fahrenheit.

Step 5:Gently scoop little balls of batter inside the oil. When they are brown on one side, flip them and allow them to continue to cook until they are browned on both sides.

Step 6: Once done, remove them from the oil and place on paper towels or a cooling rack to drain off any excess oil.
